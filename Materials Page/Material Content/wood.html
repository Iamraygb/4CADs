Wood

Description:

Wood is a natural, organic material sourced from trees. It's widely used in construction, furniture making, and crafting due to its strength, versatility, and ease of use. Different types of wood, such as hardwood and softwood, offer varying properties.

Application:

Bricks are primarily used in construction for walls, pavements, and structures like buildings, chimneys, and fireplaces. They are also used in decorative facades, garden landscaping, and outdoor paving.

Properties:

Brick is strong, durable, fire-resistant, and offers good thermal insulation. It also provides sound insulation and is relatively low maintenance. However, it's heavy and can be brittle under certain conditions, leading to cracking.

Sustainability:

Brick is considered sustainable because it's made from abundant natural materials and has a long lifespan.
